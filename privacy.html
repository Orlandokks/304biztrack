Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

This page explains what data BizTrack stores in the browser and how that data is used.

Stored Data

BizTrack stores products, orders, expenses, language preference, and cookie consent in localStorage on your device.

Purpose

The stored data keeps the dashboard, tables, CSV export, language toggle, and consent banner working between page visits.

No Third-Party Sale

The project does not sell personal data. It uses external CDNs only to load fonts, icons, and charts required by the interface.

User Control

You can clear the stored data at any time by clearing this site's browser storage.
